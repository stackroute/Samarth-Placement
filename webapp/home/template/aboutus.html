About us

Samarth uses technology to organise Skill & Job marketplace by covering large amount of population to its skill database and Job database. Samarth does what UBER did to Taxi based transportation, for the skilled people recruitment across all possible industry and service segments in India as a first goal This is done by organising Skilled people finding for a job of any industry and/or service segment as a well organised, predictable, trustable and reliable online marketplace Hence Samarth can be viewed as a large database of skilled people, jobs currently available, past placement history and outcomes from the placement history, with intelligence to identify next successful placement for either job provider or skilled people
